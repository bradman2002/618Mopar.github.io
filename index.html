Who We Are

Built on
Southern IL
Pride.

The 618 Mopar Car Club is Southern Illinois's premier gathering of Mopar enthusiasts — folks who bleed red, white, and Pentastar. From bone-stock classics to fire-breathing Hellcats, all Mopar metal is welcome here.

We're more than a club. We're a community of gearheads, shade-tree mechanics, and weekend warriors who share a passion for everything Chrysler has ever built. Whether you drive a '70 'Cuda or a 2026 Charger, if it's Mopar — you're family.

Based in the 618 area code of Southern Illinois, our members span from Carbondale to Belleville, Harrisburg to Alton. We host car shows, drag nights, and other events all year long.
